Back up selected Android folders to your own rsync target over Tailscale.

The app is designed for private, one-way Android-to-server backup workflows. It includes built-in Tailscale networking, so the official Tailscale Android app is not required. Your rsync destination can be a PC, NAS, VPS, or home server reachable through your tailnet.

Typical use cases:

• Back up photos, videos, downloads, and documents
• Push Android files to a home server, NAS, or VPS
• Run regular phone-to-server backups over your tailnet
• Keep mobile backups inside your own infrastructure

This app is intended for users who already understand rsync and operate their own backup target.
